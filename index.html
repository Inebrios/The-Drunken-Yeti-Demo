Welcome to the Drunken Yeti! We are a a fun, laid-back server looking for mature members interested in a more casual ark gameplay. Levels and early game come fast on our server, ensuring people can get to a quick, more casual end-game experience. We have boosted rates that will keep you playing, but not leave you bored after a week of being leveled from just looking at at tree.

Our collections of mods are stackable, stable, and improve on the PvE content of the game. Mutual Tribe wars are available for those that may want to duke it out with each other though. Mods like Ark Advance 10, Eco Mods, Structures Plus help make the core gameplay not too grindy, but also more fun. We also offer voting rewards, and we do have a Patreon. (Although voting is being tweaked at the moment.) We also try to have lots of events where players can win not only decent items (Longnecks, Armor, Etc.), but dino recolors (want your rex to be blue? Sure!). Our events (so far) include : Scavenger Hunts, Dodo Fights, Single Player Gauntlets (in our new arena), Last Man Standing Gauntlets, World Boss Fights, and much more to come!

At the end of the day, the admins and owner are here to listen to the community to make it a good experience for everyone. We come down hard on bullying and toxicity, and we will listen to any and all feedback as we help to shape the community in a place where everyone can hang.

You do not need to join our Discord, but it will definitely help, as that is where a lot of our communication and polls (for mods, event times, etc) occur. We do our best to reach out to those who do not have discord, but sometimes we can't guarantee that things like the MotD will be seen.

Our mornings are slow, but our evenings and afternoons, especially on the weekends and on event days, tend to bring out more players (7+). Feel free to stop by and give us a look, even if it's just to hang for a little while. This community grows starting with you!
