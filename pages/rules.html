The Drunken Yeti Rules

This page contains the rules for the ingame server and the Discord server so please familiarize yourself with them so there is no trouble!

This is a laid back server and common sense goes a long way. If you don't think you should do it, you probably shouldn't do it. We are very flexible but these rules will be enforced if they are effecting the rest of the server population in a negative, or unwanted way.





  • 1. Don't be an asshat. This is the most important rule of the server.
  • 2. Be respectful and courteous to ALL players. Not just the staff. General banter is fun and encouraged, but in a respectful way.
  • 3. No racism or sexism.
  • 4. No hacking or abusing bugs.
  • 5. This is a Pve server but you are allowed to have mutual tribe wars.
  • Please Note : Under no circumstances are you required or obligated to give breedings, items, or dinos to people you do not like. Nor are you obligated or required to speak to them. Just tell them you are sorry, you don't want to speak to them. If they attack you, or try to provoke you, please take screenshots and send it to an admin so we can deal with it accordingly. If you ever have any problems, please provide screenshots, as it makes judging things far, far easier. If possible though, please try to resolve your issues between yourselves in a calm, respectful, and courteous manner.

  • 1. No building inside of artifact caves or near the entrances of artifact caves.
  • 2. You can build on the ruins areas as long as it is not blocking things that all players should have access to.
  • 3. No pillar/foundation spamming. You CAN claim land, but only the land that you need and will use. Don't hog all of the land! Pillars or foundations that are abandoned for more than 2 days will be demo'd.
  • 4. If you need to block spawns inside your base, you are free to put pillars and foundations to block spawns, but only within/inside your base.
  • 5. No building on/blocking rich resource areas.
  • 6. On abberation, you are allowed to build near the public Transmitter (found at 34.9/50.1), but you may not block access to the Transmitter. Any bases blocking access will be demo'd to re-allow access.
  • 7. Permanent Bases are NOT allowed to be built on Viking Bay on the beach. It's okay to build there while starting off, but once you are capable of moving, you should move to allow places for new folk to start, and then eventually move out.
